myUAEU Portal

  • https://my.uaeu.ac.ae
    Your UAEU community web portal for groups, calendars, timetables, and single sign on to Webmail, Blackboard and more.

Webmail 

  • http://cloudmail.uaeu.ac.ae 
    The UAEU Microsoft Office 365 Web login page for Staff, Faculty & students, otherwise known as "OOW" - Outlook On The Web.

Password Management Self Service

  • https://selfservice.uaeu.ac.ae  
    All student, faculty and staff are responsible for taking the appropriate steps, to select and secure their passwords.

Blackboard Learning

  • https://elearning.uaeu.ac.ae
    Blackboard - UAEU Online Education system, for students and faculty members

Degree works

  • Degree works
    It is a web-based advising tool, which can be used as a road map towards students’ graduation. Degree Works helps both students and their advisors successfully navigate curriculum requirements in order to achieve success throughout their educational life. The main goal of Degree Works is to help students graduate in time.

DoIT Online Support Services

  • https://doit.uaeu.ac.ae 
    Online IT Helpdesk system. Users can submit requests & complaints, as well as track their submitted cases online. Accessible on & off campus.

Internship System

  • https://internship.uaeu.ac.ae
    The Internship Online System Login Page for Students, Faculty and Partner Organizations

Faculty Evaluation

  • https://facultyevaluation.uaeu.ac.ae
    The Faculty Evaluation system is used by Deans, Department Chairs, Faculty Members and other Administration.

CMHS Intranet 

  • http://intranet.fmhs.uaeu.ac.ae  
    Private website where CMHS faculty, staff and students can access web resources like curriculum calendar, online purchasing request, online surveys, online forms etc.

OCLC WorldCat Library

  • https://uaeu.on.worldcat.org/signin?postAuth=https://uaeu.on.worldcat.org/discovery

    Discovery System to access and download licensed E Resources: Authenticate your access with your UAEU account credentials to begin searching and downloading licensed electronic resources.
